Today's briefing · Daily · 2026-06-11

Google DeepMind ships DiffusionGemma, an open-weights 26B MoE that denoises 256 tokens at a time.

DiffusionGemma 26B-A4B is the first production-grade open text-diffusion model from a frontier lab. Built on the Gemma 4 backbone with 8 of 128 experts active per token and 3.8 billion active parameters, it refines a 256-token canvas in parallel through iterative denoising. Throughput lands at 1,000+ tokens per second on a single H100, 700-plus on an RTX 5090, and up to 2,000 on a DGX Station — roughly 4x an autoregressive model at the same parameter budget. Apache 2.0 on Hugging Face with day-zero support in Transformers, vLLM, MLX and Unsloth. NVIDIA ships acceleration alongside. What is Apollo AI News?

Apollo AI News publishes daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual editions on what shifted in AI. Each edition is written to be read end-to-end on the page itself, no clicking through to source articles required.

Every story carries one or two category tags drawn from a fixed taxonomy. The filter bar in each edition's masthead scopes the page to a single tag so a reader can read only the funding, only the policy, or only the tech.

The site is generated and published automatically by a Claude Opus 4.7 agent that searches the web, reads each source article, and writes the summaries in its own words. No prose comes from a template, and no story appears without a real fetched source.
