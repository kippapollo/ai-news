Today's briefing · Daily · 2026-06-04

DeepSeek opens its first outside round at $59B, Tencent and CATL anchor the China AI capital stack.

DeepSeek closed its debut external financing at 50 billion yuan, roughly $7.4 billion, valuing the Hangzhou lab between $52 and $59 billion. Founder Liang Wenfeng anchored the round with 20 billion yuan of his own capital. Tencent committed 10 billion yuan, battery maker CATL 5 billion, NetEase and JD.com 3 billion each. The mark is roughly six times DeepSeek's April valuation. Microsoft Build closed with Majorana 2 and Microsoft Discovery GA. Anthropic published twelve months of AI-cyber telemetry showing the share of medium-risk actors using Claude rose from 33 to 56 percent, and restructured the Claude Partner Network into a Services Track ladder. Snowflake Summit shipped CoCo, Apache Iceberg v3 and Cortex Training. Tesla took unsupervised Robotaxi to the full Austin metro on 20 active cars. Coralogix raised $200 million at $1.6 billion, Quobly closed €115 million for silicon quantum, and Apoha exited stealth with $36 million for wave-form materials AI.

What is Apollo AI News?

Apollo AI News publishes daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual editions on what shifted in AI. Each edition is written to be read end-to-end on the page itself, no clicking through to source articles required.

Every story carries one or two category tags drawn from a fixed taxonomy. The filter bar in each edition's masthead scopes the page to a single tag so a reader can read only the funding, only the policy, or only the tech.

The site is generated and published automatically by a Claude Opus 4.7 agent that searches the web, reads each source article, and writes the summaries in its own words. No prose comes from a template, and no story appears without a real fetched source.
