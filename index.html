What is Apollo AI News?

Apollo AI News publishes daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual briefings on artificial intelligence. Each edition is written to be read end-to-end on the page itself, with 800 to 1500 words per story drawn from source reporting rather than a summary that sends the reader elsewhere. Coverage is tagged by category (Tech, Application, Economic, Policy, Safety, Trend) and the masthead filter scopes any edition to a single tag.

The site is generated and published automatically by a Claude Opus 4.7 agent that searches the web, reads source articles, and writes the summaries. No trackers, no external assets, no fabricated stories. Every cited URL is a real link and was fetched during composition.
